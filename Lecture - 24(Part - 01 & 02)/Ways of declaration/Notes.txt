There are 3 ways of Declaration :
    --> let 
    --> const
    --> var

    let and const are added after ES6.(EcmaScript - 6).

Reinitialize : redeclaration of any variable...
Reassign : assigning (or changing) its value again...

Defference between ways of Declaration..

    const : 
        It can not be reassigned..
        It can not be redeclared..

    let :
        It can not be redeclared..
        But, It can be reassigned..

    var :
        It can be redeclared..
        And, It can be reassigned..
